TONMISHISHO

You are just a random stranger, helping out a ramen shop. You think it would be easy and won't be for long. but you end up at the other side of an interrogation table. You are accused for the murder of your landlord at the same ramen shop that you helped out. 

Recall the day before the murder and explore an  interesting world full of color and mystery! 

The fate of your testimony is in your hands. Find out the truth or don't...you decide
